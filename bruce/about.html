ABOUT US

It all started with a vision. To give the people of Kent the best boba that they have ever had. And with this vision, we created TruTea to meet our goals. What started as a simple idea, has turned into our way of giving back to our own community.

Amy was the one who was the first one to come up with the idea of opening up a boba shop. Growing up as an Asian American, Amy has been exposed to both cultures, and realizes the unique experiences that Asian Americans go through in their lives. She fondly remembers drinking boba with her family and friends, and sees boba as more than just a drink. She believes that boba is a staple of Asian American culture, bringing together people through a common experience.

Michael grew up around boba all of his life. As an Asian American, he and Amy shared a common cultural background. They both realized that boba is a symbolic drink for many people like them, and the power it has to bring people together. Michael has opened up his own boba shop in the past, the name being ChaTime, and now has teamed up with Amy to start TruTea. He believes that everyone should know what good boba tastes like, and strives to make sure that he can meet this goal.

At TruTea, we strive to provide the best product possible for our customer's to enjoy. We make sure that our drinks are made with only the best ingredients, created from locally produced sources. Only the best goes in our drinks, because we know that our customers desserve the best that we have to offer. This is part of our goal to make sure that every customer walks out of our store 100% satisfied with their delicious drinks. Our mission is simple, to give people the best boba that we can possibly offer!
